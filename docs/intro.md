# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This AI-native textbook is designed to guide you through the fundamental concepts of embodied intelligence, from basic principles to advanced humanoid systems.

## About This Textbook

This textbook follows the official hackathon syllabus and is structured to support Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) querying. Each chapter and lesson has been carefully crafted to provide:

- Clear learning objectives
- Comprehensive concept explanations
- Practical examples and illustrations
- Key takeaways for reinforcement

## Course Structure

The textbook is organized into 8 progressive chapters:

1. **Introduction to Physical AI** - Building foundational understanding of embodied intelligence
2. **Sensors & Perception** - How robots perceive the physical world
3. **ROS 2 – The Robotic Nervous System** - Mastering fundamental robot control
4. **Robot Simulation** - Virtual environments before real-world deployment
5. **NVIDIA Isaac Platform** - Advanced AI perception and navigation
6. **Humanoid Robot Development** - Understanding movement and interaction
7. **Vision-Language-Action (VLA)** - Integrating LLMs with robotics
8. **Capstone – The Autonomous Humanoid** - Integrating all concepts

## Getting Started

Select any chapter from the sidebar to begin your journey into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. Each chapter builds upon previous knowledge, but can also be read independently for specific topics of interest.

## Learning Approach

This textbook is designed for:

- Students and researchers in robotics and AI
- Engineers working with physical AI systems
- Anyone interested in the intersection of artificial intelligence and robotics

Each lesson includes learning objectives, concept explanations, examples, and key takeaways to ensure comprehensive understanding.
